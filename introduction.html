Fahrenheit 451

  • Author: Ray Bradbury
  • Publisher: Ballantine Books
  • Publication Date: October 19, 1953
  • Point of View: Third Person
  • Genres: Dystopian and Fiction

Overview: In a future where books are illegal and firemen burn them to keep people from thinking too much, Guy Montag has always done his job without question. But when he meets a curious young woman who makes him see the world differently, everything changes.

Montag starts to wonder if there's more to life than mindless TV/Technology and following orders. His discovery of hidden books sets him on a dangerous path, forcing him to choose between the safety of his old life and the risky freedom of knowing the truth.

Fahrenheit 451 is a thrilling story about standing up for what you believe in and the power of knowledge in a world that tries to silence it.
