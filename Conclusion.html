What We Learned

Fahrenheit 451 shows us the dangers of censorship and the value of knowledge. Montag’s journey teaches us to question the world around us and fight for what matters. The book reminds us that even in tough times, change and hope are possible.

Could It Happen Today?

In some places, books are already being banned, and people are silenced for their views. Fahrenheit 451 warns us about losing our freedom to think and learn. We must stay alert and protect the right to share ideas.

The Most Important Theme That Connects to Our Lives Today: Technology

Technology plays a huge role in Fahrenheit 451. In the story, it keeps people distracted and stops them from thinking deeply. This feels very true today. Social media, endless notifications, and screen time can make us feel disconnected from real life.

At the same time, technology has great benefits. It helps us stay connected and learn new things. But, like in the book, we need to be careful. It’s important to use technology wisely and make time for real conversations and critical thinking.
